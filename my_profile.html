Hulk Hogan

American professional wrestler

I'm Hulk Hogan, born Terry Gene Bollea,
but most people know me as the larger-than-life wrestling icon from the 1980s.
With my signature handlebar mustache, bandana, and catchphrase "Whatcha gonna do, brother?"
I became the face of the World Wrestling Federation (WWF) and helped bring professional wrestling into the mainstream.
My journey in the ring made me a pop culture phenomenon, and I took that energy into movies and television, continuing
to entertain fans around the world.
